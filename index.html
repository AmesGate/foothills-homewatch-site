What We Do

Per the National Home Watch Association, a home watch visit is defined as a visual inspection of a home or property looking for obvious issues or concerns.

Foothills Home Watch & Concierge Services is an ACCREDITED member of the National Home Watch Association.

Why Accreditation Matters

National Home Watch Association – Accredited Member

  • Insured and bonded as required
  • Background checked and vetted for business practices
  • Professional standards and ethics oversight

When you partner with Foothills Home Watch & Concierge Services, you benefit from over 20 years of professional mountain property maintenance experience.

Prior to branching out into the Home Watch industry, Kathy Crump (Owner and Lead Inspector) managed and oversaw the maintenance of a 130-unit condominium complex in Evergreen, Colorado.

We recognize the unique challenges that come with maintaining property at altitude and look forward to helping you with yours.

What We Monitor on Regular Visits

Every visit follows a documented checklist and includes photo-based reporting so nothing is missed.

  • Heating systems and filters
  • Electrical systems and breakers
  • Security systems
  • Frozen pipes and leaks
  • Unusual smells or sounds
  • Animal infestation and wildlife damage
  • Windows and doors secured
  • Water damage, mold, or mildew
  • Smoke and carbon monoxide alarms
  • Garage doors, gates, and lighting
  • Vandalism or misuse of property
  • Ice dams and exterior damage
